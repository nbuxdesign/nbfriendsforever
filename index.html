Experience Design for Smart Speakers present an interesting challenge; How can you accurately map an experience when the input method is language?

We typically see interacting with digital products as a series of very basic interactions - Taps, drags and clicks are relatively binary commands that effectively propel users forward toward a goal. Intents are easy to understand as a users end goal and follow-ups act as input fields that a device may ask you to clarify in order to complete an intent but utterances reveal a blind spot.
