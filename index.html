Wrap-up

I'm a seasoned UX designer and art director who loves turning big, messy ideas into meaningful digital experiences. Over the years, I’ve worked on everything from TVE apps to games to e-commerce platforms, always focusing on designs that feel both intuitive and visually interesting.

To me, good design is about fostering genuine connections, making life easier for users, and working hand-in-hand with others to get there.

Or more specifically:

  • Ideation and confidently presenting to stakeholders and cross-functional teams
  • Developing comprehensive UX documentation to help build a story for the project
  • Building concepts and translating them into polished prototypes
  • Building cohesive design systems that help development teams succeed
  • Grounding every decision in user insights and business objectives

GET WRECKT

I team up with stakeholders, product managers, and developers to shape a clear strategy right from the start. I’m big on open communication, experimenting early, and iterating until we’ve nailed an experience that just clicks. At the end of the day, I want my work to feel both purposeful and genuinely enjoyable to use.

By iterating early, testing with users, and refining continuously, I think any project is capable of hitting the sweet spot between the user’s delight and business results. Ultimately, it’s about making design decisions that move the needle in a meaningful way.
